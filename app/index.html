About us

Irréel Visual Art 致力於提供不同視覺具象化的設計服務，以精細寫實的視覺表現、渲染過去與未來。
The imaginary architectures rethink the city as a dream landscape, blending memories of the past with visions for the future.

Services

ARCHITECTURAL VISUALISATION
“Light, shadow, texture and color are the tools that we combine to communicate the values of architecture through an image.”
INTERACTIVE VR
Realistic Interactive Stereoscopic 3D 360-degree VR Panoramic Walk-Through Tours for marketing suites and design development for Architects, Interior Designers, and Developer.
MODELING
Advanced modeling methods allow realizing explicit, complicated 3D models of textiles, upholstered beds, sofas, and finely detailed organic shapes.
ARCHITECTURAL MOVIE
Exterior and Interior Walk-Through/Fly-Through Animations - Marvelous, exuberant, atmospheric marketing animations.
